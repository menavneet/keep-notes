Privacy Policy for Keep Notes App

Last Updated: 28 Oct 2023

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy governs the use of the Keep Notes mobile application (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). The App is designed for users to create and manage notes. The privacy of our users is of utmost importance to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to safeguard your data.

By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please refrain from using the App.

2. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following types of information:

a. User-Provided Information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, and password to enable account management and data synchronization.
  • Notes and Content: The App allows you to create and save notes, which are stored on your device and may be synchronized to our servers if you choose to enable cloud backup.

b. Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, including the device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and system logs.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about how you use the App, such as the features you access, the time and duration of your usage, and your interaction with the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

a. Providing the App's Core Functionality:

  • To allow you to create, manage, and synchronize notes across devices.
  • To improve the App's performance and features.

b. Communication:

  • To send you important service-related messages, such as account verification, password reset, and updates.

c. Analytics:

  • To analyze user behavior and trends, helping us improve the App's user experience.

4. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement reasonable safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your data.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from individuals under 13. If you believe we have collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately, and we will take appropriate steps to delete the information.

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following cases:

  • With your explicit consent.
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. If we make substantial changes, we will notify users through the App or via email. Your continued use of the App after such modifications constitutes your acknowledgment and acceptance of the changes.
